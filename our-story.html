Our Story

?We started tactup because we became tired of starting over?.

When Cam had his first baby girl all he wanted to do was make a cool birth announcement and send it to all of his loved ones. It should have been simple, but when his phone wiped out all of his contacts he was left with a handful of birth announcements and no one to send them to.

Bryan on the other hand was frustrated that his address book was incomplete, out of date, and required constant time and effort in order to keep it updated. In addition to that he didn?t even know his most important friend?s and family?s mailing addresses. What if he wanted to send flowers to Grandma?

One day while hiking up Grouse Mountain in North Vancouver, we had an epiphany:

?Imagine an address book that required minimal effort, provided concise accuracy, was always up-to-date, and most importantly was super easy to use.?

By the time we finished our climb we had mapped out our path to creating the most accurate and efficient address book on the planet.

Now, after two years of hard work, sweat, trial, error and a lot of investment, we have finally reached the summit and are ready to share tactup with the world.

The only question is:

?Are you ready to stop starting over too? If so, come join us in the connection revolution and turn your contacts into lifelong relationships.?
